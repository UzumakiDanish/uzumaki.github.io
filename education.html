MY EDUCATION

My Kindergarten Experience

My kindergarten years at Tabika Kemas Pandan Indah Fasa 2B were my first introduction to learning and discovery, filled with moments that would set the stage for my love of education. Located conveniently near my grandparents' house, it quickly became a second home—a place where friendships were made, creativity was sparked, and new skills were learned every day.

Age 5 - First Steps

At five years old, I took my first steps into the classroom. Nervous at first, I soon found a world of colors, stories, and games that made learning feel like play.

Acting Debut - "Kind-hearted Tree Feller"

I proudly represented my kindergarten in a local acting competition. I played the role of a kind-hearted tree feller, and to my surprise, we won first place! This experience helped me build confidence and discover a love for storytelling.

Final Exam - First Place

By the end of the year, I was thrilled to receive the first-place award in my final exams, a reward for all the hard work and excitement that the year had brought.

Making Friends and Learning Together

Kindergarten was where I learned to make friends, share toys, and work as a team—important lessons that I still carry with me today.
